# Laboratory Log: SSP Case Study 1 — Session 2

## 1. Objective
Compute Spatial Periodogram and Capon spectrum, estimate number of sources using MDL, and apply MUSIC algorithm to find the K directions of arrival.

## 2. Activity
Computed Spatial Periodogram as P_period = (1/M^2) * real(sum(conj(A) .* (R_hat * A), 1)) and Capon spectrum as P_capon = 1 ./ real(sum(conj(A) .* (Rinv * A), 1)). Applied MDL criterion using eigendecomposition of R_hat. Computed MUSIC pseudospectrum using noise subspace E_noise = Q(:, K_MDL+1:end).

## 3. Observations
Capon spectrum shows two dominant peaks at 2.2 and 11.7 degrees. MDL criterion gives K=5 sources, higher than the two visible Capon peaks. MUSIC with K=5 identifies five DoAs: -75.04, 2.09, 10.03, 12.92, 80.05 degrees. Users 1 and 5 near endfire directions show highest power (~19.3 dB). Users 2, 3, 4 show moderate power (~13-14 dB) and closely spaced angles.

## 4. Decisions
Used K=5 from MDL for MUSIC instead of K=2 from Capon. Capon lacks resolution to distinguish closely spaced sources near 10-13 degrees. MUSIC confirmed all five sources including weak ones.

## 5. Next Steps
- Design Capon spatial filters for each of the 5 DoAs
- Estimate QPSK symbols and decode ASCII messages
