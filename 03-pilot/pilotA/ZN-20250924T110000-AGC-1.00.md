# Spatial Spectrum: Capon and Resolution

## Core Idea
For each exploration angle, the system creates a beam
pattern, letting through that direction and nulling
everything else, generating the optimum beam pattern.

Capon formula:
P_capon(theta) = 1 / (a^H(theta) * R^-1 * a(theta))

RESOLUTION: ability to distinguish between two close
targets. To achieve resolution, R_hat must be inverted.
