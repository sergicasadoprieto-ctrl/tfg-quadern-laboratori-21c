# Laboratory Log: Survey Distribution — The 21st Century Lab Notebook

## 1. Objective
Distribute the survey among researchers and students at UAB Campus research groups to validate the documentation gap identified in Phase I.

## 2. Activity
Survey distributed via Google Forms to eleven departments at UAB Campus. Video pitch shared alongside the survey link to introduce the project and motivate participation.

## 3. Observations
113 valid responses collected between 2026-05-08 and 2026-05-11. Most respondents were Senior Researchers and PhD Students. Mean reproducibility difficulty score: 3.08/5.

## 4. Decisions
Survey closed on 2026-05-11 with 113 responses. Data exported to Excel for Python processing.

## 5. Next Steps
- Process raw data with Python (Google Colab)
- Generate Power BI dashboard
- Publish results to GitHub Pages in HTML
